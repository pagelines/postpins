== Description ==

PostPins allows you to pull posts into your site, in a similar way to Pinterest. 
It has robust options and cool effects; such as the ability to have infinite scrolling. 

To use simply drag in the PostPins section to the templates you'd like to use it on. For example, a page or even your blog template.

*FREE with <a href="http://pagelines.com/plus">PageLines Plus</a>.
